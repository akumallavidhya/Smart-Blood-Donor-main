Do You Know?

Blood cannot be made artificially, only one person can save another. But sadly, every year a large number of people are dying due to lack of necessary blood in an emergency. At present Bangladesh needs only 9 lakh bags of blood every year. But in this populous country people are still dying due to lack of blood. This demand for blood can be easily met if adults in all parts of our country are made aware of the need and benefits of blood donation. There can be no greater joy than to bring a dying patient back to his loved ones in good health. Stay by the side of dying patients in need of emergency blood. For those who wish to donate blood, please register as a blood donor on this website. Patients will find you in case of emergency blood transfusion. Happy Blood Donating.

Some Mis-conceptions

1. There is no pain at all when donating blood. It just feels a little uncomfortable to pierce the needle. 2. Health will deteriorate after blood donation this is a misconception. In fact, donating blood reduces the risk of heart disease and prevents excessive iron storage in the body. 3. People with diabetes cannot donate blood this is also a misconception. If considered eligible for health examination, a person with diabetes can donate blood as long as the person's blood glucose level is within the recognized limits. 4. Blood cannot be donated because of high blood pressure this is also a misconception. Blood can be given if the blood pressure is between 160 systolic and 100 diastolic. 5. After donating blood, you will not feel any discomfort or faint. Many people have misconceptions about this.

Who can donate blood?

Any healthy adult, both male and female, can donate blood. Men can donate safely once in every three months while women can donate every four months. And also You are aged between 18 and 65. You weigh at least 50 kg.
